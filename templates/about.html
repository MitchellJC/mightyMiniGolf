About Us

We have been having a blast putting holes and accomplishing goals since 1989. We have 16 unique and creative holes across our beautiful 10 acre course. After working up an appetite from playing minigolf, take a break and try some of our famous homestyle burgers, also conveniently located next to our airconditioned laser tag facility. Just wait until the kids will putt their first hole in one, they will be wanting to come back every weekend.
