Our Food Bar

Our food bar is located by the laser tag facility as you walk into the Mighty Mini Golf grounds. We have been serving our famous homestyle burgers and golden crunchy chips for over 20 years. Children eat for half price on weekdays and ask the front desk about our daily specials.

Due to Covid-19 we require seated ordering through our app. To order just scan the QR code on your table, your food will be brought right to you!
